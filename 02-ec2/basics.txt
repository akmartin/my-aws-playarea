EC2: Virtual Machines in the cloud

Pricing:
On demand - Allows you to pay a fixed rate by the hour or second without any commitment

Reserved - Provides you with a capacity reservation, and offer a significant discount on hourly charge for an instance. Contract terms are 1year to 3year terms .

Spot - Enables you to bid whatever price you want for instance capacity, providing for even greater savings if you applications have flexible start and end times
If the spot instance is terminated by EC2, you will not be charged for a partial hour of usage. However if you terminate the instance yourself, you will be charged for any hour that the instance ran.  

Dedicated Hosts - Physical EC2 server dedicated for your use. Dedicated Hosts can help you reduce costs by allowing you to use you existing server-bond software licenses
